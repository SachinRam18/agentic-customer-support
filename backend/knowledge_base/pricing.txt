CloudBox Pricing Plans and Subscription Information
====================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
CloudBox offers three subscription tiers designed to fit individuals, small teams, and large enterprises. All plans include end-to-end AES-256 encryption, real-time sync across devices, and access to the web interface and native desktop/mobile apps.

A free tier with 10 GB of storage is available with no credit card required.

---

PLAN: Starter
Price: $199 per month | $159 per month billed annually ($1,908 per year — save 20%)
Storage: 100 GB
Single File Upload Limit: 5 GB
Version History: 30 days
Support SLA: 4 hours response time (business hours)
Team Features: Basic sharing and collaboration
Best For: Individual users and freelancers

Starter Plan includes:
- 100 GB of secure cloud storage
- Up to 5 GB per single file upload
- 30-day file version history
- Standard email and chat support
- Desktop apps for Windows, macOS, and Linux
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Real-time sync across devices
- File sharing with view-only or edit links
- AES-256 client-side encryption
- 30-day trash recovery window

---

Storage: 1 TB (1,000 GB)
Single File Upload Limit: 50 GB
Version History: 90 days
Support SLA: 2 hours response time, available 24/7
Team Features: Advanced collaboration and admin controls
Best For: Small to medium teams and power users

Professional Plan includes everything in Starter, plus:

- Up to 50 GB per single file upload
- 90-day file version history

---

ANNUAL BILLING DISCOUNT
------------------------
All plans offer a 20% discount when billed annually:
- Starter: $199/mo → $159/mo annually
- Professional: $499/mo → $399/mo annually
- Enterprise: $999/mo → $799/mo annually

Switching to annual billing is prorated from your current monthly balance. The discount applies immediately on switch.

---

FREE TIER
---------
Storage: 10 GB
Price: Free, no credit card required
Limitations: No priority support, no version history beyond basic, basic file sharing only
Note: After subscription cancellation, storage reverts to the 10 GB free tier.

UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE RULES
-----------------------------
- Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remaining billing days.
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle renewal date.
- No cancellation fees ever — downgrade or cancel anytime.

---

TRIAL AND REFUND WINDOW
------------------------
CloudBox does not offer a traditional free trial for paid plans. However, all first-time paid plan purchases carry a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied within 7 days of your first purchase, you may request a full refund. This applies only to first-time purchases and is not available on renewals or add-ons.

---

PAYMENT METHODS ACCEPTED
-------------------------
- Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Debit Cards: All major debit cards
- UPI (India): Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
- Net Banking: All major Indian and international banks
- International cards with automatic currency conversion

All payment data is processed under PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance. CloudBox does not store raw card numbers.
